Help center
Guidance for the moments that matter.
⚠️ Have an urgent issue?
Storm damage, flooding, HVAC failure — tap for priority scheduling. Management and your handyman are notified immediately.
What to do when…
⚠️ Storm damage
After a storm — roof & exterior
Don't go on the roof yourself. Document damage with photos, check for interior water intrusion. Most insurance claims require professional documentation within 48–72 hours.
⚠️ Flooding
Water in your home
Turn off main water supply, cut power to affected areas, document everything. Call Haven — water damage worsens quickly.
🌡 HVAC
AC stops working in Texas heat
Check thermostat and breaker first. Replace filter if clogged. If the unit won't run, schedule a priority visit.
❄️ Seasonal
Preparing for DFW winters
Insulate exposed pipes, know your shutoff valve, schedule a Haven winterization check in November.
🚨 Urgent request
📷 Add photos of the damage
🔔 Submitting notifies Haven management and Marcus immediately by text & email.

Haven tips & tricks
🌡 Seasonal
Prep your AC before the Texas heat hits
Change your filter, clear debris from the outdoor unit, and schedule a tune-up before temps climb past 100°F.
💬 Guidance
When to call us vs. wait for your next visit
Active leaks, power loss, HVAC out in extreme weather, or gas smell — call now. Slow drips, one bad bulb, or a sticky door can wait for your visit.
⚡ Savings
How preventive care saves you $3,500 a year
A clogged filter makes your HVAC fail years sooner. A slow drip wastes 3,000 gallons a year. Haven catches these before they get expensive.
